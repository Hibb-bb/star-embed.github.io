Random Embeddings serve as a baseline comparison method in the Star-Embed benchmark. These are randomly generated embeddings that provide a lower bound for performance evaluation, helping to demonstrate the effectiveness of learned representations:

  • Architecture: Random vector generation
  • Parameters: None (random generation)
  • Purpose: Baseline comparison
  • Input: Time series data
  • Output: Random embeddings of same dimension
  • Features: Uniform random distribution
