🏗️ Model Architecture

Astromer-1 is a transformer-based model specifically designed for astronomical time series analysis. It leverages attention mechanisms to capture long-range dependencies in irregularly sampled light curves and handles the unique challenges of astronomical data:

  • Architecture: Transformer with attention mechanisms
  • Specialization: Astronomical time series
  • Input: Irregularly sampled light curves
  • Output: Time series embeddings and predictions
  • Features: Handles missing data and irregular sampling
